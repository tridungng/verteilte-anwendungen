Introduction

Security misconfiguration occurs when security settings are not adequately defined in the configuration process or maintained and deployed with default settings. Basically, any poorly documented configuration changes, default settings, or a technical issue across any component in your endpoints could lead to a misconfiguration.

The purpose of this tool

This tool is built as part of the class "Verteilte Anwendung" together with a research paper as the final project. With this project, we hope to bring a ground understanding and awareness to a serious security vulnerability, which is often overseen.

Our Challenges

We present 3 challenges, which differ from one another in difficulty (Challenge 1 is the easiest and Challenge 3 is the hardest) and variation, are to demonstrate security vulnerabilities, which resulted from misconfigurations. Hints and tips will be given during the challenges to assist you. The estimated time to complete each challenge, ranging from easy to difficult, is respectively 5, 10 and 15 minutes.

This learning platform to demonstrate Security Misconfigurations was created by Dominik Nalewaja, Luca Lumare Martinez, Tri Dung Ngo und Rimaz Issa.
